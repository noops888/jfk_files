SUBJECT: - . Resignation of ARRB ExDir
REFERENCE:

Effective this Friday, July 24, Jeremy Gunn has resigned from his position as Executive Director
to the Assassination Record Review Board. He is taking a position with US Institute for Peace.

The Board announced the following appointments:

Executive Director: Laura Denk (formerly head of the ARRB's FBI review team)
Deputy ExDir: Tracy Shycoff (formerly Associate Director for Administration)
General Counsel: Ron Haron ( formerly Associate General Counsel)

There are no changes in the ARRB's CIA team. Michelle Combs, Special Assistant for Research and
Review, will continue to handle special requests, and Robert Skwirot, Chief of the CIA review team. will
continue to focus on the document review.
