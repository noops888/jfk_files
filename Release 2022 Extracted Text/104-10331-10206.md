SUBJECT : JFK Assassination Records-Request for
Review of SCI Documents

1. Attached for your review are two documents from the
JFK Assassination Records Collection which contain equities
of the Directorate for Science and Technology. As you know,
the Assassination Records Review Board (ARRB) is in the
process of reviewing assassination-related documents for
release to the public. In order to assist in this process,

declassification and indicate by yellow highlighting those
areas that you determine should be redacted. The earlier
version of these documents, released in 1993 and 1994
respectively, are attached for reference.

contact me at S31825 or Mr. Jim Hanrahan at S31809. We
would appreciate receiving your response by 1 December 1997.
