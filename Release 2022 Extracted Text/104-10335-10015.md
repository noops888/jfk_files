Dear John:

We would like to request all files, documents, and records that relate to Sylvia Duran's

first arrest and interrogation in Mexico City immediately following the assassination.

We have seen many references in documents to Sylvia Duran's "ten-page statement"

that she gave to the Mexican police, but we have not been able to locate the statement

itself. We consider this record important in understanding events pertaining to Oswald

in Mexico City.

For your convenience, we have attached several of the documents from the collection

that reference the statement. We request that you locate this original statement and any

other records that provide any information regarding the statement.

Thank you for your assistance.
