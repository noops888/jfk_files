1.

BACKGROUND: CIA informal Memo CI/897, undated, subj as above,

informed ACSI that Mrs. Lillian Sloboda, wife of SP-5 Vladimir 0. Sloboda,

defectee to the Soviets in Aug 60, has returned to London to reside w/her

mother, Mrs. Sutcliffe, in Halifax, England. Mrs. Sloboda is travelling with

her daughter, age three, while her other two children are still in Moscow.

The BIS (British Intelligence Service) is attempting to determine whether

Mrs. Sloboda has left USSR permanently or whether she is in England on

a temporary visit.

BIS intends to interview her after she is settled, and

has req any questions the US would like answered. DA G2347 made USAREUR

aware of Mrs. Sloboda's return to England and req any EEI for passage to BIS.

Their answer

was furnished in DA IN 220659, 12 Apr 62, giving EEI considered

of

primary security interest. EEI recd fr USAREUR was passed to CIA for transmittal

to BIS. Req

made that any info obtained by BIS would be of interest to this

office.

2. CURRENT: CIA Itr, subj as above, 23 Jul 62, informed this office

that requested replies fr BIS on questions submitted for Mrs. Sloboda not

yet recd. This office will be advised by CIA immediately upon receipt of

any info fr BIS on the subj. Ltr, subj as above, 30 Jul 62 fr this office

advised USAREUR of CIA's message.
