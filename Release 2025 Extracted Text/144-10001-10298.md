CAMBODIAN CONDOLENCES TO DEAN RUSK ON DEATH OF PRESIDENT

FR Phnom Penh: HUOT SAMBAI, Secretary

of State for Foreign Affairs

TO Washington: DEAN RUSK

Msg NI: 310/ DP

23 Nov 63 1305

Priority

The Royal Government learned with shock the news of the

attack against President KENNEDY and his death.

The Royal Government offers its respects to the mortal

remains of the illustrious deceased, whom Cambodia regarded with

sincere admiration for his actions for peace and for the relaxation

of international tensions.
