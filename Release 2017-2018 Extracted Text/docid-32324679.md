MEDELVINE have been located in Tel Aviv, Israel, this

matter is being placed in a pending inactive status

re-opened and an appropriate letterhead memorandum

submitted requesting the Legat, Tel Aviv to again

verify their residence in Tel Aviv.

Appropriate stops have been placed with the

Immigration and Naturalization Service, New York, on

3/3/71, for both OGUL and MEDELVINE.

letterhead memorandum from the Legat, Tel Aviv dated

12/29/71, to the affect that MEDELVINE resides on

Trumpeldor Street, number not known, Tel Aviv, Israel.

to the Chicago Office to the effect that DON JO MEDEL-

was in Israel and did not intend to return to the United

die in prison.
