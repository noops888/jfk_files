had conversation with JIMMY FAZIO at the Fireside Steak House,

Fort Lauderdale, Fla., on the night of 12/30/62, at which time

fact that New

start some action.

out of Chicago". FAZIO stated that he would telephone TRAFFICANTE

on 12/31/62 and would ask him to visit in Cocoa Beach, indicating

he could expect a visit within the week.
