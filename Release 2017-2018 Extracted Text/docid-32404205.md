Suggested Press Guidance on Release

of CIA

Records on Kennedy Assassination

1.

On 16 August, CIA released to the National Archives

approximately 23,000 pages of declassified records related

to the assassination of President John F. Kennedy.

records are comprised of documents and other materials

contained in CIA's file on Lee Harvey Oswald.

This is in

addition to approximately 12,000 pages from the oswald file

that were transferred to the National Archives in 1992.

of the records in the Oswald file that can be declassified--

more than 90% of that file--have now been released.

2. A large volume of additional declassified records

related to the assassination and investigation are to be

released shortly.

The records have been declassified under

the requirements of the President John F. Kennedy

Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992.
