## Page 2

25 FEB 1976

Honorable James A. Wilderotter

Associate Counsel to the President

The White House

Washington, D.C. 20500

Dear Mr. Wilderotter:

Dr. Athan Theoharis, a researcher for the Senate Select Conmittee on

Intelligence Activities, recently conducted research at the John F.

Kennedy Library and, in the course of his research, requested copies

of a number of classified documents.

Copies of documents he selected,

classified through Secret, are enclosed.

Three documents classified

Top Secret will be forwarded to you after their delivery to us by the

Armed Forces Courier Service.

The documents enclosed include FBI investigative information on

individuals and organizations, information on internal security of

the Dominican Republic, and information on U.S. internal security programs.

DI. Theoharis and John Elliff have requested that these documents be

reviewed and conveyed to the Committee as soon as possible. Would you

please advise us of the outcome of your review.

Sincerely,

Macabo

RICHARD A. JACOBS

Deputy Assistant Archivist

for Presidential Libraries
