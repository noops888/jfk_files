UNITED FRONT FOR NATIONAL LIBERATION

INTERNAL SECURITY - CUBA

REGISTRATION ACT - CUBA

On April 16, 1963, FELIPE VIDAL SANTIAGO was

interviewed in the custody of Immigration and Naturaliza-

tion Service (INS) authorities, Miami, where he advised

he is the Secretary of the United Front for National

Liberation,

and was being held by INS authorities for

violation of an order restricting him to Dade County,

Florida.

MM T-1, who is closely associated with VIDAL and

who has furnished reliable information in the past, advised

on April 16, 1963, that evening VIDAL was released by INS

authorities

to the custody of his attorney, CHARLES R.

ASHMANN.

MM T-I explained that ASHMANN is a Miami Attorney who

represents a number of Cuban nationals in Miami who are

Involved in violations of INS and U.S. Customs laws. per-

taining to

armed raids and expeditions against the present

government of Cuba.

MM I-I said that ASHMANN stated VIDAL is facing

an exclusion hearing by INS authorities, and that ASHMANN

would represent VIDAL at this hearing.

MM T-1 explained that ASHMANN was working on a

petition of belligerency for VIDAL, who will attempt to

use a belligerency status as a method of continuing plans

for raids against Cuba.
