AR - CONSPIRACY

ReRomlet 9/28/67.

Dr • NINO MENDOLIA, Chief, Squadra Mobile,

(Flying Squad), Police Headquarters, Palermo, Sicily,

telephonically advised this office on December 7;

1967, that there are no new developments with regard

to the impending trial of the 17 persons charged with

aggravated criminal conspiracy in 1965.

Dr . MENDOLIA

stated that it is unlikely the trial will be held

before the Spring of 1968.

According to Dr: MENDOLIA, there are some

indications that Instructional Judge, ALDO VIGNERI,

of the Palermo Tribunal, may order the issuance of

additional warrants in January, 1968; however, the

persons who will be named are not known at this time.

•Dr. MENDOLIA Said he will notify Legat promptly of

any pertinent developments in this regard

Legat will follow this matter with its

sources and report any pertinent developments to

the Bureau.
