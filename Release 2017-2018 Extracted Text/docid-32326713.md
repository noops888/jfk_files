Reference is made to this Bureau's memorandum

dated June 14, 1976, captioned as above, requesting approval

for the utilization

of a body recorder and/or transmitting

device on the person of a confidential source of our

San Francisco Office.

Deputy Assistant Attorney General

Jay C. Waldman approved request on June 14, 1976, for a

30-day period.

Our San Francisco Office has been conducting an

investigation into the criminal activities of Salvatore

Amarena, Aldena "Jimmy the Weasel" Fratianno, and other

individuals. Amarena, Fratianno,

and the others are all

notorious West Coast organized crime figures and they are

allegedly involved in Interstate Transportation of Stolen

Property, Extortionate Credit Transactions, and Hobbs Act

violations.

Our San Francisco Agents have been contacting a

confidential source who was present when these individuals

had conversations regarding their criminal activities.

The conversations occurred at Amarena's place of business

and also at the confidential source's apartment in

San Francisco.
