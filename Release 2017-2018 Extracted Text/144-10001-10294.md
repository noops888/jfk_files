States where he represented. the

Government at: the

newsmen that

president LYNDON JOHNSON intends to convoke representatives

from the South American countries.

United States Secretary of State DEAN RUSK and that they took

up various matters of a bilateral nature regarding relations

between

and the United States.

had a briet informal conversation with President DE GAULLE and

with the French Ambassador to the United States. • •
