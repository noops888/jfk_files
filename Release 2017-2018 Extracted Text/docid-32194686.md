On 12/9/63 SA 648-S furnished the writer a written

report which is located in 134-363A-173, and which contains

the following information:

RENE

On Saturday, November 23, 1963, at about 6:00 p.m.

SANDOVAl was seen arriving at'

the home of JOHN STANFORD.

STANFORD spoke of the tragic news of President

KENNEDY's death and stated that the man who killed President

KENNEDY was not a member of the Communist Party.

STANFORD stated that he had never heard of this man

who killed KENNEDY.

STANFORD stated that Governor CONNALLY

will most likely get re-elected after the accidentin which

he was also shot during the killing of President KENNEDY.

STANFORD's wife stated that now after the killing of

President KENNEDY that the Communist Party was being blamed

for it, and that JOHN SIANFORD will have a hard time with the

U. S. Government.

JO STANFORD also stated that she was worried about

STANFORD's life being in danger.

STANFORD claimed that he was not worried nor afraid

about being harmed.

STANFORD gave SANDOVAL his copies of:

1. The Worker, November 17, 1963

2.

The Mid-Week Worker, November 15, 1963

3.

Political Affairs, November, 1963.
