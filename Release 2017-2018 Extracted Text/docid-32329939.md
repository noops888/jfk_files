on 3/5/64, Mr. CHARLES VALE, INS, Las Vegas, Nevada,

advised SA THOMAS H. ALT of the following information, which he

said came from an unidentified source who he felt was reliable:

VALE said the source reported that the money for the

sale of the Horseshoe Hotel and Casino had already changed hands,

although the paper work concerning this sale had not been completed.

He said that recently EDWARD LEVINSON had gone to the Beverly

Wilshire Hotel in Los Angeles to meet with

"DOC" STACHER, and to

deliver the money for the sale of the Horseshoe. The source told

VALE that some of this money went on into Mexico, although the

source had no further information concerning this transfer of

funds to Mexico.
