DETAILS:

On November 14, 1969, T-1 advised that her
husband had just returned from Seattle, Washington, on
a business trip and had learned that JOE GASPARAVICH
and his family had been robbed at his home approximately
one year ago by two armed men wearing masks. The two
men broke into GASPARAVICH's home, forced their daughter
to remove her clothes, tied her up, then waited for
GASPARAVICH and his wife to return.

T-1 recalled subject DOLAN discussing such
a robbery in her presence indicating how he had undressed
the girl and ate a bowl of ice cream while he waited for
the family to return. Informant earlier advised that
she had seen DOLAN in posséssion of masks and guns.
