Dear Bob:

Your letters of April 12, 1978 requested further

information on Lee Harvey Oswald.. The Army has now

completed its file search. The search revealed that

an intelligence dossier on him was destroyed in 1973.

A copy of the deletion record is attached.

It should

be noted

that the dates on the tracing index are

written in the military style of day, month, year.

Using additional information communicated by your

staff to Ms. Judy Miller, the Army has also completed

an extensive search for the November 22, 1963 telegram

requested in the January 26, 1978 letter. The telegram

was not located.
