Interview: Sergio Arcacha Smith, former New Orleans delegate for

the C.R.C., was interviewed at his

place of residence.

Subject states while he was a delegate for the C.R.C. in

New Orleans, David Ferrie came to him and offered his services,

which he accepted. He had never seen or met LHO and engaged in

no activities where an assassination attempt of President Kennedy

was planned of discussed. He left New Orleans in 1962, was em-

ployed by the Penton I.P.C. at 2106 West Loop South, Suite 501,

Houston, Texas, at (713) 961-7841.

Subject is willing to be deposed and offered to submit to

a lie detector test to prove his non-involvement to certain alle-

gations presented against him.

When queried if he knew "Thomas Beckham," subject states

that he never met him and that he never knew any person named

"Rose Cheramie," "Mabel Mercedes" or "Emilio Santana."
