64-13 39th Avenue

WOODSIDE, I. I., NOT.

Now 24. 1957.

My Dear Ursula and Steve s

Thark you so very mich for your wedding picture, wedg

you remembering us.

Your little daughter must be a darling and I am sure you

her very much.

Please forgive my delay in writing to you both. I do

mey meet some time.

Our 2 boys are now 17 yrs and 21 yrs.

We sometimes ronde

the years have to go by so quickly.

Our oldest son Paul is now overseas in the Army, anderer

Carl will graduate fron figh School this coming Spring.

May

I say to you steve enjoy your little girl whald she

My very best wishes to you and your dear wife at this meanort of

the year.

Sincerely Yours

Margaret Richter.
