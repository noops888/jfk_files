Our San Francisco office advises that a confidential source is

furnishing them information regarding the current criminal activities

of Salvatore Amarena, Aladena "Jimmy the Weasel" Fratianno, and other

individuals. Amarena and the others are all notorious West Coast

organized crime figures and allegedly they are involved in Interstate

Transportation of Stolen Property, Extortionate Credit Transactions,

and Hobbs Act violations.

This confidential source is present when these individuals have

conversations regarding their criminal activities. The conversations

occur at Amarena's place of business in San Francisco and also the

confidential source's apartment.

The confidential source has advised that she is willing to

cooperate and will testify in this matter. The confidential source is also

agreeable to the installation of a microphone in her apartment to record

her conversations between Amarena, Fratianno, and others.

Strike Force Attorney John Emerson advised that he feels the

confidential source's conversations with these individuals should be recorded

and he feels no entrapment is involved.

Your authority is requested for our San Francisco office to install

a microphone in the apartment of this confidential source in San Francisco,

California, for a period of 30 days based on the consent furnished by the

confidential source.
