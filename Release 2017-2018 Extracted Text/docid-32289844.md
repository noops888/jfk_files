and politicians Chicago Division is desirous of instituting

photographic surveillances.

A sixteen millimeter movie camera equipped with

a normal fixed focus lens with

an automatic electronic

controlled diaphragm. If this is not available, an eight

millimeter camera with through the lens automatic electronic

controlled diaphragm could be utilized. This camera to be

concealed in a modern small attache or

similar type case.

The camera to be mounted in a fixed position within the case!

The lens of the camera to be at an opening at one end of the

case.: A plunger type cable release mounted on the

bottom of the case to control the shutter from the outside.

Chicago anticipates using

the equipment for a period

of sixty days of receipt of the equipment.
