HUGO GONZALEZ LAJONCHERE On November 17, 1959, advised

he bad been in touch wita SInGIO SANJENIS CABARROCAS and explained

to SANJENIS that ROLANDO MASFERRER desired to have SANJENIS con-

tact him. GONZALEZ LAJONCARRE Stated he had informed SANJENIS

concerning MASFERRER's desires and that SANJENIS did intend to

contact MASFEBRER.

GONZALEZ LAJONCHERE pointed out that he be-

Ileves that SANJENIS definitely will not partake of any counter-

revolutionary plot in which MASFERRER is involved. GONZALEZ

LAJONCHERE stated that it was his belief that SANJENIS might

give some indication to MASFERRER of cooperating with him but

that he doubted very much whether SANJENIS would become involved

in any counterrevolutionary movenent headed by MASFERRER.

ZALEZ LAJONCHERE stated, however, that he did not belief that

SANJENIS would inform Cuban authorities concerning anything he

might learn from MASFERRER.
