CRIMINAL INFORMANT COVERAGE

It is contemplated that SU-370-C, whose identity

is known to the Bureau, will be utilized concerning the

investigation of ROSELLI at the Desert Inn Hotel.

Although

this informant is not acquainted with ROSELLI it is believed

that the informant will be able to furnish pertinent

information concerning ROSELLI's activities if ROSELLI

continues to maintain residence at that hotel.

Mr. ROBERT CANNON, Manager, Tropicana Hotel, has

furnished reliable information in the past concerning

ROSELLI.. CANNON has also furnished reliable information

concerning other investigations at the Tropicana Hotel.

CANNON is in a position to furnish pertinent information

concerning ROSELLI's connections with MONTE PROSER and the

Tropicana Hotel. CANNON is well acquainted with ROSELLI.

Contact will be maintained with

CANNON concerning the

activities of ROSELLI.

JACK BROOKS, who occupies the position of Doorman

at Tropicana Hotel is well acquainted with ROSELLI and has

furnished reliable information in the past concerning ROSELLI.

BROOKS is connected with Nevada Concessions Inc., at Tropicana

Hotel and is in a good position to furnish information concern-

ing ROSELLI's activities.

HOWARD CAPPS, Professional Golfer, in charge of the

Desert Inn Hotel Country Club, Las Vegas, Nevada, of which

ROSELLI is a member, has been contacted in the past concerning

ROSELLI.

CAPPS is well acquainted with ROSELLI and is in an

excellent position to report information concerning ROSELLI's

activities at the Desert Inn Hotel and at the Desert Inn Hotel

Country Club. Contact will be maintained with CAPPS.

It has been determined that the following potential

criminal informants are well acquainted with ROSELLI and in

amposition to furnish pertinent information concerning his

activities at Las Vegas. Contact will be maintained with these

sources:

PCI

Desert Inn Hotel Casino.
