PG T-1

inhabitants of Villa Rosa, Italy.

PG T-2

This individual is a former associate of La Rocca who can

furnish information relating to several of La Rocca's past

financial interests.

PG T-3

This individual is a long-time numbers writer in New Kensington,

Pennsylvania, who is personally familiar with most of the

members of the Mannarino organization and with some of the

gamblers from the Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, area.

This individual is a thirty-four year old man who is a life-

long resident of the New Kensington, Pennsylvania, area who

visits gambling establishments in the New Kensington and

Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, areas on a frequent basis.

PG T-5

This individual is extremely familiar with prostitution-type

activities in the Pittsburgh area and has a speaking acquaintance

with the members of both the La Rocca-Genovese and Mannarino

organizations.

PG T-6

This individual is well acquainted with gambling activities

and participants in the Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, area.
