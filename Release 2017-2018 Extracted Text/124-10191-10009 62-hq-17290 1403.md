Dear MI. Attorney General:

By letter dated August 9, 1977 to the Attorney

General, the Select Committee on Assassinations re-

quested

delivery of certain reports pertaining to the

robbery of the Bank of Alton, Illinois. As this matter

is of extreme importance to the Committee's inquiry

into the circumstances surrounding the death of Dr.

Martin Luther King, JI., the Committee respectfully

requests delivery of the remaining portions of this

file on a priority basis.

Additonally, the Committee requests that the

Federal Bureau of Investigation review this file with

the understanding that the Committee intends to enter

these materials as a part of the Committee's public

record.

I sincerely thank you for your cooperation and

assistance extended to the Martin Luther King Task

Force over the course of this investigation.
